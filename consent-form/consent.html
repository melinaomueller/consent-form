Vanderbilt University — Information Sheet

Title:                           Mechanisms and abilities in high-level perception

Principal Investigator:    Dr. Isabel Gauthier — Psychology Department

This research study, conducted at Vanderbilt University by Prof. Isabel Gauthier, is designed to help our understanding of how people perceive and recognize objects. You may first be asked to provide information about yourself (e.g., sex, age, race, native language, highest level of education completed).

The study can include the following tasks (but possibility not all of them):

Surveys including questions about your personality, your attitudes and your experience in a certain domain.
Perceptual tasks including visual and/or auditory tasks asking you to make simple decisions about images (e.g., letters, words, faces, familiar or unfamiliar objects) or sounds (e.g., bird songs).
Cognitive tasks with images, words, letters or numbers that test your knowledge of a domain or your abilities to solve different kinds of puzzles.

Your participation is voluntary and you may discontinue participation at any time without penalty. Participation involves no more than minimal risk. One possible risk of participation is loss of confidentiality. All reasonable efforts will be made to keep your personal information confidential but absolute confidentiality cannot be guaranteed. You are under no obligation to complete any other study.

Confidentiality: The only identifying information kept based on your participation in this study is your ID on this platform (either Prolific or MTurk). Disclosures that you agree to in this document are not protected. This includes putting research data in the medical record or sharing research data for this study or future research. Disclosures that you make yourself are also not protected. The information in this study will be used only for research purposes and in ways that will not reveal who you are. Federal or state laws may require us to show information to university or government officials (or sponsors) who are responsible for monitoring the safety of this study. You will not be identified in any publication from this study. Your data, without any personal information attached, may be shared in public data repository to provide the research community with open access to scientific data.

Compensation: If you satisfactorily complete the study, you will receive the amount listed in the information for this study on the website (Prolific or MTurk). The estimated duration of the task you are asked to complete is listed on the platform, and you will be compensated at a rate no less than $8/hr.

Questions or Concerns: You can ask questions about this research study now or any time during the study by e-mailing Isabel Gauthier at Isabel.gauthier@vanderbilt.edu. If you have questions about your rights as a research participant or feel that you have not been treated fairly, please contact the Vanderbilt University Human Research Protection Program Office at 615-322-2918, or toll-free 866-224-8273
